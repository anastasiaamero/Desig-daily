Design Daily is a curated journey through the history of visual design, architecture, and material culture. Each day features a new work that shaped the world we live in.

From the functionalism of Bauhaus to the bold graphics of contemporary studios, this archive celebrates the thinkers and makers who transformed how we see and interact with the world.

Collection

The collection spans over a century of design history, from the foundational works of the early 20th century to the digital innovations of today. Each entry includes context about the designer, the era, and the lasting impact of the work.

Categories

Works are organized into categories including Bauhaus, Graphic Design, Product Design, Architecture, Typography, and Editorial. Browse by category to discover connections across disciplines and time periods.

Mission

Design Daily exists to make design history accessible and engaging. By featuring one work each day, we encourage thoughtful exploration rather than overwhelming consumption. Design is not just about aesthetics — it's about how we live, work, and communicate.
